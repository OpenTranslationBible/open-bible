# Joshua Chapter 16

## Joshua 16:1

The lot for the descendants of Joseph began at the Jordan near Jericho, east of the springs, and proceeded into the wilderness, ascending through the hill country to Bethel.

## Joshua 16:2

From Bethel, it moved to Luz, and crossed to the border of the Archites at Ataroth.

## Joshua 16:3

It then descended westward to the territory of the Japhletites, reaching as far as Lower Beth Horon and extending to Gezer, ending at the sea.

## Joshua 16:4

Thus, the descendants of Joseph, Manasseh and Ephraim, received their inheritance.

---

## Joshua 16:5

This was the territory allotted to the Ephraimites, according to their clans: the boundary of their inheritance extended from Ataroth Addar in the east to Upper Beth Horon.

## Joshua 16:6

The boundary then proceeded westward at Michmethath on the north, curving eastward to Taanath Shiloh, and passing it to the east of Janoah.

## Joshua 16:7

It descended from Janoah to Ataroth and Naarah, touched Jericho, and emerged at the Jordan.

## Joshua 16:8

From Tappuah, the border moved westward to the Kanah Ravine and ended at the sea. This was the inheritance of the tribe of the Ephraimites, according to their clans.

## Joshua 16:9

Additionally, there were towns and their villages set apart for the Ephraimites within the inheritance of the Manassites.

---

## Joshua 16:10

They did not expel the Canaanites living in Gezer; therefore, the Canaanites continue to live among the Ephraimites to this day, and they became subject to forced labour.
