# Joshua Chapter 19

## Joshua 19:1

The second lot fell to the tribe of Simeon according to their families; their inheritance was situated within the portion of the tribe of Judah.

## Joshua 19:2

Their portion comprised Beersheba (or Sheba), Moladah,

## Joshua 19:3

Hazar Shual, Balah, and Ezem,

## Joshua 19:4

Eltolad, Bethul, and Hormah,

## Joshua 19:5

Ziklag, Beth Markaboth, and Hazar Susah,

## Joshua 19:6

Beth Lebaoth, and Sharuhen; thirteen towns with their villages;

## Joshua 19:7

Ain, Rimmon, Ether, and Ashan; four towns with their villages—

## Joshua 19:8

and all the villages surrounding these towns as far as Baalath Beer (Ramah of the Negev). This was the inheritance of the tribe of Simeon according to their families.

## Joshua 19:9

The inheritance of Simeon was taken from the portion of Judah, because Judah’s share was too large for them. Thus, the Simeonites received their inheritance within the territory of Judah.

---

## Joshua 19:10

The third lot came out for the tribe of Zebulun according to their families. The boundary of their inheritance extended as far as Sarid.

## Joshua 19:11

Their border went up westward to Maralah, touched Dabbesheth, and reached the brook near Jokneam.

## Joshua 19:12

It turned eastward from Sarid toward the sunrise to the border of Kisloth Tabor, continued to Daberath, and ascended to Japhia.

## Joshua 19:13

From there it proceeded eastward to Gath Hepher and Eth Kazin, and came out at Rimmon, curving toward Neah.

## Joshua 19:14

The boundary circled on the north to Hannathon and ended at the Valley of Iphtah El.

## Joshua 19:15

Included were Kattath, Nahalal, Shimron, Idalah, and Bethlehem; twelve towns with their villages.

## Joshua 19:16

These towns and their villages constituted the inheritance of Zebulun according to their families.

---

## Joshua 19:17

The fourth lot came out for the tribe of Issachar according to their families.

## Joshua 19:18

Their land encompassed Jezreel, Chesulloth, Shunem,

## Joshua 19:19

Hapharaim, and Shion, and the settlement of Anaharath,

## Joshua 19:20

Rabbith, and Kishion, and the settlement of Ebez,

## Joshua 19:21

Remeth, En Gannim, En Haddah, and the settlement of Beth Pazzez.

## Joshua 19:22

The boundary touched Tabor, Shahazumah, and Beth Shemesh, and ended at the Jordan; sixteen towns with their villages.

## Joshua 19:23

These towns and their villages were the inheritance of the tribe of Issachar according to their families.

## Joshua 19:24

The fifth lot came out for the tribe of Asher according to their families.

## Joshua 19:25

Their territory included Helkath, Hali, Beten, and Akshaph,

## Joshua 19:26

Allammelech, Amad, and Mishal; on the west the boundary touched Carmel and Shihor Libnath.

## Joshua 19:27

It turned eastward toward Beth Dagon, touched Zebulun and the Valley of Iphtah El, and went north to Beth Emek and Neiel, passing Kabul on the left.

## Joshua 19:28

It went to Ebron, Rehob, Hammon, and Kanah, as far as Greater Sidon.

## Joshua 19:29

The boundary then turned back toward Ramah and went to the fortified city of Tyre, turned toward Hosah, and ended at the Mediterranean Sea in the region of Achzib.

## Joshua 19:30

Included were Ummah, Aphek, and Rehob; twenty-two towns with their villages.

## Joshua 19:31

These towns and their villages were the inheritance of the tribe of Asher according to their families.

## Joshua 19:32

The sixth lot came out for the tribe of Naphtali according to their families.

## Joshua 19:33

Their boundary started from Heleph and the large tree in Zaanannim, passing Adami Nekeb and Jabneel to Lakkum, and ending at the Jordan.

## Joshua 19:34

The boundary turned westward to Aznoth Tabor and came out at Hukkok. It touched Zebulun on the south, Asher on the west, and the Jordan on the east.

## Joshua 19:35

The fortified towns were Ziddim, Zer, Hammath, Rakkath, and Kinnereth,

## Joshua 19:36

Adamah, and Ramah, and the settlement of Hazor,

## Joshua 19:37

Kedesh, and Edrei, and the settlement of En Hazor,

## Joshua 19:38

Yiron, Migdal El, Horem, Beth Anath, and Beth Shemesh; nineteen towns with their villages.

## Joshua 19:39

These towns and their villages were the inheritance of the tribe of Naphtali according to their families.

## Joshua 19:40

The seventh lot came out for the tribe of Dan according to their families.

## Joshua 19:41

The territory of their inheritance included Zorah, Eshtaol, and Ir Shemesh,

## Joshua 19:42

Shaalabbin, and Aijalon, and the settlement of Ithlah,

## Joshua 19:43

Elon, and Timnah, and the settlement of Ekron,

## Joshua 19:44

Eltekeh, and Gibbethon, and the settlement of Baalath,

## Joshua 19:45

the cities of Jehud, Bene Berak, and Gath Rimmon,

## Joshua 19:46

Me Jarkon and Rakkon, along with the region facing Joppa.

## Joshua 19:47

After the Danites lost their territory, they went up and attacked Leshem, captured it, and settled there, renaming it Dan after their ancestor.

## Joshua 19:48

These towns and their villages were the inheritance of the tribe of Dan according to their families.

## Joshua 19:49

Once the land had been divided into its portions, the Israelites granted Joshua son of Nun an inheritance among them,

## Joshua 19:50

as the Lord had commanded. They gave him the town he requested—Timnath Serah in the hill country of Ephraim. He built up the town and settled there.

## Joshua 19:51

These are the territories that Eleazar the priest, Joshua son of Nun, and the heads of the tribal clans of Israel assigned by lot at Shiloh in the presence of the Lord at the entrance to the tent of meeting. Thus, they completed the division of the land.
