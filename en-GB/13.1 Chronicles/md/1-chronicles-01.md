# 1 Chronicles Chapter 1

## 1 Chronicles 1:1

Adam, Seth, and Enosh,

## 1 Chronicles 1:2

Kenan, Mahalalel, and Jared,

## 1 Chronicles 1:3

Enoch, Methuselah, and Lamech,

## 1 Chronicles 1:4

Noah, along with his sons:

> Shem, Ham, and Japheth.

---

## 1 Chronicles 1:5

Japheth's sons included:

> Gomer, Magog, Madai, Javan, Tubal, Meshech, and Tiras.

## 1 Chronicles 1:6

Gomer's sons were:

> Ashkenaz, Diphath, and Togarmah.

## 1 Chronicles 1:7

Javan's lineage included:

> Elishah, Tarshish, Kittim, and Rodanim.

---

## 1 Chronicles 1:8

Ham's sons included:

> Cush, Mizraim, Put, and Canaan.

## 1 Chronicles 1:9

Cush's sons were:

> Seba, Havilah, Sabta, Raama, and Sabteca.  
> And Raama's sons were:  
> Sheba and Dedan.

## 1 Chronicles 1:10

> Cush was the father of Nimrod;  
> he became a mighty figure on the earth.

## 1 Chronicles 1:11

Mizraim was the ancestor of the people of

> Lud, Anam, Lehab, Naphtuh,

## 1 Chronicles 1:12

> Pathrus, Casluh (from whom the Philistines came), and Caphtor.

## 1 Chronicles 1:13

Canaan was father to

> Sidon, his firstborn, and Heth,

## 1 Chronicles 1:14

and the Jebusite, the Amorite, and the Girgashite,

## 1 Chronicles 1:15

and the Hivite, the Arkite, and the Sinite,

## 1 Chronicles 1:16

and the Arvadite, the Zemarite, and the Hamathite.

---

## 1 Chronicles 1:17

The sons of Shem included:

> Elam, Asshur, Arpachshad, Lud, Aram,  
> The sons of Aram were  
> Uz, Hul, Gether, and Meshech.

## 1 Chronicles 1:18

Arpachshad was father to Shelah,  
and Shelah was father to Eber.

## 1 Chronicles 1:19

Eber had two sons:

> one was named Peleg, for in his days the earth was divided;  
> and his brother was named Joktan.

## 1 Chronicles 1:20

Joktan's descendants included:

> Almodad, Sheleph, Hazarmaveth, Jerah,

## 1 Chronicles 1:21

Hadoram, and Uzal, and Diklah,

## 1 Chronicles 1:22

Ebal, Abimael, and Sheba,

## 1 Chronicles 1:23

Ophir, Havilah, and Jobab.  
These were all the sons of Joktan.

---

## 1 Chronicles 1:24

> Shem, Arpachshad, and Shelah,

## 1 Chronicles 1:25

> Eber, Peleg, and Reu,

## 1 Chronicles 1:26

> Serug, Nahor, and Terah,

## 1 Chronicles 1:27

> and Abram, who is also known as Abraham.

---

## 1 Chronicles 1:28

> Abraham's sons were:  
> named Isaac and Ishmael.

## 1 Chronicles 1:29

These are their generations:

> Ishmael's firstborn was Nebaioth, followed by Kedar, Adbeel, and Mibsam,

## 1 Chronicles 1:30

Mishma, Dumah, Massa, Hadad, and Tema,

## 1 Chronicles 1:31

Jetur, Naphish, and Kedemah.  
These were Ishmael's descendants.

---

## 1 Chronicles 1:32

The sons of Keturah, Abraham’s concubine, whom she bore:

> Zimran, Jokshan, Medan, Midian, Ishbak, and Shuah.  
> Jokshan's sons were:  
> Sheba and Dedan.

## 1 Chronicles 1:33

Midian's sons were:

> Ephah, Epher, Hanoch, Abida, and Eldaah.  
> These were all Keturah's sons.

---

## 1 Chronicles 1:34

Abraham fathered Isaac.

> Isaac's sons were:  
> Esau and Israel.

---

## 1 Chronicles 1:35

Esau's sons were:

> Eliphaz, Reuel, and Jeush; Jalam, and Korah.

## 1 Chronicles 1:36

Eliphaz's sons were:

> Teman, Omar, Zephi, Gatam, Kenaz, Timna, and Amalek.

## 1 Chronicles 1:37

Reuel's sons were:

> Nahath, and Zerah; Shammah, and Mizzah.

---

## 1 Chronicles 1:38

Seir's sons were:

> Lotan, Shobal, and Zibeon; Anah, Dishon, Ezer, and Dishan.

## 1 Chronicles 1:39

Lotan's sons were:

> Hori and Homam; and Timna was Lotan’s sister.

## 1 Chronicles 1:40

Shobal's sons were:

> Alian, Manahath, Ebal, Shephi, and Onam.  
> And Zibeon's sons were:  
> Aiah and Anah.

## 1 Chronicles 1:41

Anah's son was:

> Dishon.  
> And Dishon's sons were:  
> Hamran, Eshban, Ithran, and Cheran.

## 1 Chronicles 1:42

Ezer's sons were:

> Bilhan, Zaavan, and Jaakan.  
> Dishan's sons were:  
> Uz and Aran.

---

## 1 Chronicles 1:43

These are the kings who ruled in the land of Edom before any king reigned over the Israelites:  
Bela the son of Beor, and his city was named Dinhabah.

## 1 Chronicles 1:44

Upon Bela's death, Jobab the son of Zerah from Bozrah succeeded him as king.

## 1 Chronicles 1:45

After Jobab's death, Husham from the land of the Temanites assumed the throne.

## 1 Chronicles 1:46

When Husham passed away, Hadad the son of Bedad, who defeated Midian in the territory of Moab,  
became king; his city was named Avith.

## 1 Chronicles 1:47

Upon Hadad's death, Samlah from Masrekah took the throne.

## 1 Chronicles 1:48

Upon Samlah's death, Shaul from Rehoboth by the River succeeded him as king.

## 1 Chronicles 1:49

Following Shaul's death, Baal-hanan the son of Achbor took the throne.

## 1 Chronicles 1:50

When Baal-hanan passed away, Hadad became king;  
his city was named Pai,  
and his wife was Mehetabel, the daughter of Matred, the daughter of Mezahab.

---

## 1 Chronicles 1:51

Then Hadad died.  
And the chiefs of Edom were:  
chief Timna, chief Aliah, and chief Jetheth,

## 1 Chronicles 1:52

chief Oholibamah, chief Elah, and chief Pinon,

## 1 Chronicles 1:53

chief Kenaz, chief Teman, and chief Mibzar,

## 1 Chronicles 1:54

chief Magdiel, and chief Iram.  
These were the leaders of Edom.
